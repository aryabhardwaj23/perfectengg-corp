About Us

Delhi-Based Industry Leader in Chlorine Safety

Perfect Engineering Corporation is an ISO 9001-2008 certified manufacturer headquartered in Naveen Shahdara, Delhi, with a production facility in Sahibabad Industrial Area, Ghaziabad. We design, manufacture, and supply critical chlorine handling and safety equipment for water treatment plants, power stations, fertilizer plants, and chemical industries across India.

In-House Manufacturing
State-of-the-art production facility with precision engineering and quality control at every stage.
Certified Quality
ISO 9001-2008 certified processes ensuring consistent product quality and international standards compliance.
Dedicated Support
Expert technical team providing installation, commissioning, and after-sales service across India.
Pan-India Presence
Serving clients across 15+ states with timely delivery and comprehensive project execution capabilities.

Our Products

Complete Range of Chlorine Safety Equipment

From vacuum-operated chlorinators to corrosion-resistant FRP systems — engineered for reliability in India's most demanding environments.

Vacuum Type Chlorinator
Direct & Solution Feed Systems

Precision vacuum-operated chlorinators for safe, accurate chlorine dosing in water treatment plants, swimming pools, and industrial processes.

  • Capacity: 2 g/hr to 10 kg/hr
  • Operating vacuum: 250–350 mm Hg
  • Wetted parts in PVC/CPVC
  • Direct & solution feed options
  • Built-in vacuum regulator
VCC-2 VCC-10 VCC-200 VCC-1000 VCC-10000
Chlorine Leak Absorption System
Emergency & Continuous Scrubbing

FRP scrubbing systems with caustic soda neutralisation for safe containment of chlorine leaks in storage and handling areas.

  • Handling capacity: 25 to 500 kg/hr
  • Fan capacity: 100 to 10,000 m³/hr
  • FRP construction for corrosion resistance
  • Automatic & manual operation modes
  • CPVC/FRP tower packing
CLS-10 CLS-25 CLS-100 CLS-500
Chlorine Leak Detector
Electrochemical Sensing Systems

Multi-point electrochemical chlorine gas detectors with alarm and auto-activation of absorption systems for comprehensive facility safety.

  • Detection range: 0–10 ppm / 0–20 ppm
  • Response time: < 30 seconds
  • Visual & audible alarms
  • Relay output for system interlock
  • Multi-point monitoring (up to 16 zones)
CLD-1 CLD-4 CLD-8 CLD-16
Industrial FRP Blowers
Centrifugal & Axial Flow

Corrosion-resistant FRP blowers for chemical fume exhaust, scrubbing systems, and industrial ventilation in aggressive environments.

  • Capacity: 100 to 50,000 m³/hr
  • Static pressure: 50–1,500 mm WC
  • Speed: 960–2,900 RPM
  • Material: FRP / MS / SS
  • MOC: Polypropylene, FRP, HDPE
FRP-CF FRP-AF PP-BL SS-BL
FRP Ducting
Round, Rectangular & Custom Profiles

Hand-laid and machine-wound FRP ducts for chemical fume extraction and ventilation in corrosive atmospheres — lightweight and durable.

  • Diameter: 100 mm to 2,000 mm
  • Wall thickness as per IS/BS standards
  • Joints: flanged, socket & spigot
  • Temperature: up to 120°C continuous
  • Custom profiles and fittings available
FRP-RD FRP-SQ FRP-EL
FRP Dampers & Butterfly Valves
Flow Control for Corrosive Media

FRP and CPVC butterfly valves and dampers for precise flow regulation of corrosive gases and liquids in chemical process industries.

  • Size range: 25 mm to 1,200 mm
  • MOC: FRP, CPVC, PP, PVDF
  • Manual, gear & pneumatic actuation
  • Temperature: –20°C to 150°C
  • Pressure rating: up to 10 kg/cm²
BFV-FRP BFV-CPVC DMP-FRP

Applications

Industries We Serve

Our equipment is deployed in the most demanding industrial environments — from municipal water treatment to nuclear power stations — ensuring safe chlorine handling and corrosion-resistant operations.

Water Treatment
Municipal WTPs, STPs, and industrial water treatment facilities requiring precise chlorine dosing.
Power Generation

Pharmaceuticals
Pharma manufacturing facilities needing sterile water chlorination and process gas handling.
Fertilizer Plants

Effluent Treatment
